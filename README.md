# G-DDJR-FLARM
Display and FLARM for G-DDJR

In 2023 my friends and I went to Devon and Somerset Gliding Club for the competition enterprise they were hosting that summer. We had been talking to another aircraft owner about the upcoming requirement for all aircraft to have an electronic conspicuity device fitted. The official devices produced by the likes of Garmin and FLARM run into the many hundreds of pounds - the owner we were speaking to had said he built his based on this GitHub project - https://github.com/lyusupov/SoftRF

My friends turned to me and asked if I would be able to build them one - which I said yes to. This was a bit of a saga going back and forth with the manufacturer as the orginal T-Beam supreme we had ordered wasn't holding it's charge with. This then lead me down the route of making _another_ abused XLR plug to go from the glider's battery to a 12V-5V USB charge adaptor. This would allow the T-Beam or a phone to be charged while in flight. Once we had the new board from the manufacture, this resolved all the power issues we were having.

Fast forward to 2025 and during the late autumn, the guys asked if it would be possible to have a display mounted in the cockpit to allow for greater situational awarenesss. This was in the form of the SkyView EZ https://github.com/lyusupov/SoftRF/wiki/SkyView-EZ which went together much more smoothly than the transponder. The trickies thing about this half of the project was designing the lowest profile enclosure for the display as possible. The original files would have been too large in their 1960s vintage glider.

As of spring 2026, the display and transponder have been reporting accurate position for other aircraft when G-DDJR is airborne.
